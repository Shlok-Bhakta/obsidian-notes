a [[Differentiation]] rule

#### c = any constant (number)
$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left(c\right)\ =\ 0
$$

#### m = slope
$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left(mx\right)=m
$$

#### n = number
$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left(x^{n}\right)=nx^{n-1}
$$
